The Follow The Music Game typically involves remembering and repeating a sequence of instruments or tones. Here's a basic guide:


1. Start: Press the game's start button.
2. Watch and Listen: Pay attention to the sequence of instruments or tones that is played.
3. Repeat: Repeat the sequence by pressing the corresponding buttons in the same order.
4. Progress: The game will add a new element to the sequence. Repeat the entire sequence, including the new addition.
5. Continue: Keep repeating and remembering the sequence as it grows longer.
6. Mistakes: If you make a mistake, the game will signal it, and you have to start over.
7. Winning: The goal is to successfully repeat the increasingly longer sequences.
